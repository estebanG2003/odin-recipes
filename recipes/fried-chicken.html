Description

This crispy fried chicken features juicy, marinated meat coated in a perfectly seasoned, golden-brown crust.
It's a classic comfort food that's crunchy on the outside, tender on the inside, and packed with bold, savory flavor.

Ingredients

    üçó For the Chicken:
    • 1 whole chicken (cut into 8 pieces) or ~1.5 kg of drumsticks/thighs
    • 2 cups buttermilk (or milk + 1 tbsp lemon juice as a substitute)
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp paprika (optional)
    üçû For the Breading:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
    üç≥For Frying:
    • Vegetable oil (canola, peanut, or sunflower oil work well)

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken
    • In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with buttermilk, salt, pepper, and paprika.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best for juicy, flavorful chicken).
  2. Prepare the Breading
    • In a large bowl or shallow dish, mix flour with all the spices.
  3. Coat the Chicken
    • Remove chicken from the marinade (let excess drip off).
    • Dredge each piece in the flour mixture, pressing to adhere well.
    • Optional: For extra crunch, dip it back into the buttermilk, then into the flour again (double coating).
  4. Heat the Oil
    • Pour about 2-3 inches of oil into a deep skillet or Dutch oven.
    • Heat to 175¬∞C (350¬∞F). Use a thermometer if possible.
  5. Fry the Chicken
    • Carefully place a few pieces of chicken in the oil (don't overcrowd).
    • Fry for about 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through (internal temp: 75¬∞C / 165¬∞F).
    • Drain on a wire rack or paper towels.
  6. Serve
    • Let the chicken rest 5 minutes before serving.
    • Serve with coleslaw, mashed potatoes, cornbread, or your favorite sides.
